About GentleHandsCare

At GentleHandsCare, we believe that every senior deserves to age with dignity, comfort, and independence in the place they love most—their own home.

Our mission is to provide compassionate, professional home care services that enhance the quality of life for elderly individuals while giving families peace of mind.

We focus on personalized care plans designed around each individual's unique needs, ensuring comfort, safety, and respect at every step. Our certified caregivers are not just trained professionals—they're compassionate companions dedicated to making a positive difference.
