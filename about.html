About GentleHandsCare

Committed to providing compassionate, professional care that honors dignity and enhances quality of life for seniors.

Our Story

GentleHandsCare was founded with a simple yet powerful belief: every senior deserves to age with dignity, comfort, and independence in the place they call home.

For over 15 years, we've been providing compassionate, professional home care services to families across the United States. What started as a small team of dedicated caregivers has grown into a trusted organization serving hundreds of families.

Our journey began when our founder witnessed the challenges faced by their own aging parents. They saw firsthand how difficult it was to find quality, trustworthy care that treated seniors with the respect and compassion they deserved. This experience sparked a mission: to create a care service that families could trust completely.

Today, we're proud to be a leading provider of home care services, known for our rigorous caregiver training, personalized care plans, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Caregiver Training Process

We invest in comprehensive training to ensure your loved ones receive the best care possible

1

Rigorous Screening

Background checks, reference verification, and in-depth interviews to ensure only qualified, trustworthy individuals join our team.

2

Comprehensive Training

40+ hours of initial training covering personal care, safety protocols, dementia care, emergency response, and communication skills.

3

Certification & Licensing

All caregivers obtain required certifications including CPR, First Aid, and specialized care credentials.

4

Ongoing Education

Regular training updates, workshops, and continuing education to stay current with best practices in senior care.
